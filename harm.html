Impact of Computer Viruses

  • 💾 Data Loss: Viruses can delete or corrupt important files.
  • 💳 Financial Theft: Some viruses steal banking details and passwords.
  • 🐌 Slow Performance: Infected systems run much slower.
  • 📡 Spread to Others: Viruses can use your device to infect others.
  • 🔒 Ransom Demands: Some viruses lock files until you pay money.
  • 👀 Privacy Invasion: Spyware viruses watch your online activity.
