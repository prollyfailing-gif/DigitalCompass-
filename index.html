SecureSight — Malware Awareness

This is a demo awareness site about viruses and malware. It explains how attackers use them against people and raises awareness to stay safe online.

⚠️ Example Threats

  • Phishing: Fake login forms that steal your credentials.
  • Ransomware: Encrypts files and demands money.
  • Spyware: Secretly collects your data and activity.
  • Cryptojacking: Uses your device to mine cryptocurrency without permission.

✅ How to Protect Yourself

  • Don’t click unknown links or attachments.
  • Keep your device and software updated.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA.
  • Install trusted antivirus/antimalware software.

Share this site to spread awareness and keep others safe.
